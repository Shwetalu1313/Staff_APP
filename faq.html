Top Questions

Common actions staff members ask about when starting the day or resolving issues.

How do I submit a leave request?

Open Services, choose HR, select Leave Request, then enter leave type, dates, and your cover plan before submitting.

Where can I check my ticket status?

Go to Workspace and open the Tickets area. Active requests show their latest owner, priority, and current stage.

How do I find the latest company policy?

Use Services and open InfoHub. Search by policy name and confirm the revision date before using the document.

How can I switch between light and dark mode?

Use the theme button in the top navigation. Your preference is saved automatically for the next visit.

Where do I open company apps like Mail, Docs, or Jira?

Use the apps launcher in the top navigation or open the Apps page to launch internal and external work tools.

What should I do if I need urgent access support?

Open Services and jump to the Support Desk section, then raise an IT request with the impact, affected system, and deadline so it can be prioritized quickly.

Need a direct route?

Use the shortest path based on what you need to complete.

  • Leave, claims, and forms: Services
  • Approvals and tickets: Workspace
  • Tools and launchers: Apps
  • Urgent help desk issues: Services Support Desk
